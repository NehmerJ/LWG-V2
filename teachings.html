Knowing God


Is it possible to know God? Is there a way to know what He thinks or feels? Can I really know His will? Is knowing God just a bunch of facts or is there something more? What are the consequences of not knowing Him? We are addressing these and more questions in our new series: KNOWING GOD. We share God's plan of covenant relationship with Him as with marriage through Jesus Christ. And one of the key elements is distinguishing God's part and my part. Join us in this adventure, and share it with others if you have found answers and want others to experience the same. You can subscribe to our channel from here and reach us for questions or comments, we love to hear testimonies around the world!
