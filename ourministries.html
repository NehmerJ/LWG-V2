Jack and Nola Nehmer
Ministries

"Whether it is a home meeting or a dirt floor garage
we try to help churches get established
and function as a solid body of Christ."


The Nehmers have been missionaries since 1990 ministering in over 7 countries. Whether it is a home meeting or a dirt floor garage we try to help churches get established and function as a solid body of Christ. We believe the Word of God and a personal relationship with God as Father and Savior is the only true way to eradicate the curse of sin (Romans 6). This is the reason that our Lord Jesus Christ has sent us into the world to make disciples of Him through the preaching of the gospel and working of signs and miracles to confirm that Word (Matt 28:18-20; Mr 16:15-20).

God’s plan is to set the captive free, give sight to the blind and reconcile all who will believe with Himself (2 Cor 5:17-21; Isa 61:1-2). In God’s grace, we press on in this ministry to help each other to hear, believe and practice the truth of the Word of God to be set free from the lies that hold creation captive (Jn 8:31-36). Creation has been crying out for the sons of God to be revealed as we walk in the Spirit of God in love (Rom 8:12-39). Only motivated by love and God’s goodness, will people be brought to repentance and salvation that is offered in Jesus Christ (Rom 2:4; 2 Cor 7:9-11; Mr 1:14-15).



Discipleship

DISCIPLESHIP IS FATHERHOOD


This is our Discipleship structure, based on the Word of God​.
We want to establish relationships in the body of Christ and
become all of us a House of Prayer, interceding for the Nations.

WORSHIP

As one grows in their relationship with God so does the expression of worship as one learns to walk in the Spirit of God. We help people grow in their relationship with God through the House of Prayer which manifests daily in reading, meditation, and prayer with the Word of God.

DISCIPLESHIP

We use seminars to reach multitudes to reach denominations, congregations and ministry groups. As we grow in personal relationship with the church leadership and congregations we work in a deeper level of contact with classroom and one on one personal development. This helps each person grow beyond the level of relationship to follow Jesus and help others do the same.

INTERCESSION

The power of Intercession that Christ Jesus has given to each believer is manifest in Intercessory Prayer and through the Preaching of the Word of God as we put ourselves in the gap between man and God. This aspect is expression of authority that our Lord has given to destroy the works of Satan and set the captives free.
